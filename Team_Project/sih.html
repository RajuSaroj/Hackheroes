Overview

Smart India Hackathon (SIH) is a nationwide initiative by the Government of India to provide students a platform to solve pressing problems of society, organizations, and industries. Benefits of Participating in SIH

  • Provides an opportunity to work on real-world problems.
  • Chance to win attractive prizes and recognition.
  • Opportunity to interact with top mentors and industry leaders.
  • Enhances problem-solving, teamwork, and innovation skills.
  • Get internships or job offers from leading companies.

Registration Process

  1. Team Registration: Form a team of 6 members, with at least one female member.
  2. Team Leader: The team leader must register the team on the SIH portal.
  3. Idea Submission: Submit your idea based on the problem statements provided.
  4. Shortlisting: Shortlisted teams will get a chance to present their solutions in the SIH finals.

SIH 2024 Timeline

  • Idea Submission: February 2024
  • Shortlisting Announcement: March 2024
  • Grand Finale: July 2024
